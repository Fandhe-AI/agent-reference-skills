# NVIDIA DGX Spark Hardware Support

Standard support channel for DGX Spark hardware issues, available through the NVIDIA customer support portal.

## Usage

- Open a new support ticket (requires log in to the support portal).
- Chat live with a support agent.
- View and track existing tickets.
- Ask the community on the NVIDIA DGX Spark Forums.

## Notes

- Community forums are for peer/staff discussion, not a substitute for an official support ticket.
