# Getting Support

Overview of the support channels available for NVIDIA DGX Spark: hardware support, AI Enterprise support, enterprise manageability documentation, security/vulnerability response, and the field diagnostic software.

## Notes

- Support channels differ depending on whether the system is covered by standard hardware support or by an NVIDIA AI Enterprise—DGX Spark entitlement.
- Enterprise manageability topics (fleet lifecycle integration, cloud-init custom installation) are documented separately outside this support page (`enterprise-manageability.html`).
