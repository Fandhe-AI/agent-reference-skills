# NVIDIA AI Enterprise—DGX Spark Support

Enterprise-level support for DGX Spark, requiring a specific NVIDIA AI Enterprise—DGX Spark entitlement.

## Usage

- Open a new support ticket through the NVIDIA AI Enterprise support portal (requires log in).
- Chat live with a support agent.
- View and track existing tickets.

## Notes

- Other NVIDIA AI Enterprise entitlements do not automatically include this product or its support; the entitlement must specifically cover DGX Spark and be listed on the entitlement certificate.
