# Connectivity and I/O

Rear panel ports and connectors available on the DGX Spark.

## Rear Panel Components

- Power button
- 4x USB Type-C (one for power delivery)
- 1x HDMI 2.1a display connector
- 1x RJ-45 Ethernet connector (10 GbE)
- 2x QSFP Network connectors (ConnectX-7)

## Notes

- One of the four USB Type-C ports is used for power delivery.
- The two QSFP connectors are driven by the ConnectX-7 Smart NIC.
