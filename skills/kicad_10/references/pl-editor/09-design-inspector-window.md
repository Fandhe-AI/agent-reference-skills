# Design Inspector Window

The Design Inspector presents a table of every item in the drawing sheet and their properties.

## Signature / Usage

Open via: **Inspect** → **Show Design Inspector**

## Features

- Lists all drawing sheet items in a structured table with their properties
- Selecting an item in the inspector simultaneously selects it on the editing canvas
- The item remains selected after the Design Inspector is closed

## Notes

- Useful for locating specific items in complex drawing sheets without clicking on the canvas
- Provides an alternative selection method to direct canvas interaction
- Works alongside the Properties Editor for reviewing and editing item details
