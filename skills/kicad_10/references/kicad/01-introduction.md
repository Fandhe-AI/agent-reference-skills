# Introduction

KiCad is an open-source software suite for creating electronic circuit schematics and printed circuit boards. It supports integrated workflows (schematic and PCB designed together) as well as standalone workflows for specialized applications.

## Overview

KiCad runs on all major operating systems and supports PCBs with up to 32 copper layers. It includes complementary utilities:

- PCB calculator
- Gerber viewer
- Integrated SPICE simulator

## System Requirements

KiCad functions on diverse hardware configurations, but optimal performance requires a dedicated graphics card and display with 1920×1080 or higher resolution.

For current specifications, see: https://kicad.org/help/system-requirements/

## Notes

- KiCad is free and open-source
- Suitable for designs of varying complexity, from simple 2-layer boards to complex multi-layer designs
