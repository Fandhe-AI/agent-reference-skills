# Post-Incorporation Setup

Steps and services available after Atlas incorporation: banking, payments, fundraising, and ongoing tax compliance.

## Banking Partners

| Partner | Available Before EIN | Requirements |
|---------|---------------------|--------------|
| Stripe Treasury | Yes | Stripe dashboard |
| Mercury | Yes | Legal address (registered agent address accepted) |
| Brex | Yes | C Corp only; at least one beneficial owner with US address |
| Rho | Yes | At least one beneficial owner with US address |
| Novo | No (after EIN) | At least one beneficial owner with US SSN and US address |

Note: Stripe, Brex, Mercury, Rho, and Novo are fintech companies; banking services are provided through bank partners and are not directly FDIC-insured.

## Payments

- **$2,500 Stripe credit** is available for the first year after incorporation.
- Payments can be enabled 1–2 business days after incorporation, before EIN receipt.
- Supported: Payment Links, Invoicing, US card payments.

## Fundraising (SAFEs)

- C Corporation only.
- Atlas uses the Y Combinator post-money SAFE template with valuation cap.
- Board authorization is required before issuing SAFEs (Atlas generates a Cooley-drafted board letter via DocuSign).
- Funding methods: ACH (1 business day, free), wire ($2 USD, same day), Stripe payment balance (immediate, free).

## Tax Obligations

### Annual Deadlines

| Entity | Deadline | Obligation |
|--------|----------|-----------|
| C Corp | March 1 | Delaware franchise tax |
| C Corp | April 15 | Federal corporate income tax |
| C Corp | October 15 | Extended federal filing deadline |
| LLC | March 15 | Partnership tax return |
| LLC | June 1 | Delaware annual tax ($300) |
| Both | January 31 | W-2 / 1099 (if employees or vendor payments) |

Late filing penalty: $200 + 1.5% monthly interest (Delaware).

## Notes for Indian Founders

Indian residents (FEMA definition: 182+ days in India in prior financial year) must complete additional steps:

1. Establish an Indian LLP before the US entity (typically 2 weeks; minimum 2 LLP partners required).
2. Open an Authorized Dealer (AD) Bank account with ODI services.
3. After Atlas incorporation: obtain UIN for the US subsidiary from RBI.
4. Submit Form FC and Form A2 to AD Bank before share purchase.
5. Submit share certificates to AD Bank within **6 months** of the transfer date.

Recommended service providers: Inkle (incorporate@inkle.io) for LLP formation; Cooley (atlas-cooley@stripe.com) for US legal matters.
