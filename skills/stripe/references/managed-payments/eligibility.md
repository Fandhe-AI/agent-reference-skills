# Managed Payments Eligibility

Requirements for using Stripe Managed Payments: supported business locations, eligible product types, and ongoing performance standards.

## Options / Props

### Supported Business Locations

| Region | Countries |
|--------|-----------|
| North America | CA, US |
| Europe | AT, BE, BG, CH, CY, CZ, DE, DK, EE, ES, FI, FR, GB, GI, GR, HR, HU, IE, IT, LI, LT, LU, LV, MT, NL, NO, PL, PT, RO, SE, SI, SK |
| Asia Pacific | AU, HK, JP, SG |

Connect platforms, Express accounts, and platform-managed accounts are not eligible.

### Eligible Product Types (digital only)

| Product Type | Examples |
|---|---|
| Software & games | SaaS, video games |
| Digital media | Audiobooks, eBooks, digital magazines, audio/video content, digital images |
| Education | Online courses, training |
| Web services | Web hosting, AIaaS |

Physical goods, professional services (consulting, development, 1-on-1 coaching), and in-person events are not supported.

### Buyer Countries

195+ countries and regions. Restricted: Ascension Island, China, Cuba, Iran, Kosovo, North Korea, Russia, Syria, Tristan da Cunha.

## Notes

- Products must be fully automated with no human-intervention delivery
- You must own all necessary rights and licenses for the digital product
- Assign an eligible tax code to each product (labeled as Managed Payments–eligible in the dashboard)
- Maintain a low chargeback rate; Stripe may issue refunds within 60 days of purchase to reduce chargebacks
- Comply with regional cooling-off periods and consumer protection laws
- If your product is ineligible, use [Stripe Tax](https://docs.stripe.com/tax) for compliance instead
